━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  V1 — DO THIS FIRST (ship order):
  1. Seed School 1 + School 2 content (without this, the app has nothing to show)
  2. Wire coin awards on the backend (lesson complete, quiz pass)
  3. Complete ProfileScreen dark theme + stats
  4. Run all DB migrations on production
  5. TestFlight beta
  6. Fix beta bugs → App Store submission

  V2 — BUILD IN THIS ORDER (impact vs effort):
  1. Savings Vault      — high impact, teaches core concept, relatively simple
  2. Weekly Events      — drives daily retention, tables already exist
  3. Push Notifications — critical for retention, easy with Expo
  4. Schools 3–5        — more schools = more reason to stay subscribed
  5. Budget Calculator  — completes the "earn/spend/save/budget" loop
  6. Avatar System      — adds fun/personalization, drives store engagement
  7. Mini Games         — highest effort, highest engagement ceiling
  8. AI Financial Coach — depends on bot-service being stable
  9. Investment charts  — nice to have extension of existing feature
  10. Partner Deals     — revenue upside, lowest development effort
